ABOUT

My name is Matthew Fard, also known as Fardy. I am a 24 year old Open Format DJ based within Manchester and open to travel. I have been DJing for 5 years and have played in various clubs and bars around the UK. I have a passion for music and love to entertain people. I play a wide range of music genres, from House to Hip Hop, RnB to Throwbacks, I can cater for any event.
